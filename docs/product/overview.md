# Overview

## Context
Individuals who are interested in managing their personal finances and need a clear and centralized view of their financial data.
## Target User
Persons who want control over their finances but struggle with fragmented financial data.
## Problem
Users cannot easily understand their financial situation due to scattered data, hard-to-read bank statements, and multiple accounts or credit cards to manage.
## Solution
HawkLedger consolidates financial data into a single, structured view, enabling users to track and analyze their transactions.
## Value Proposition
A simple and centralized way to understand and control personal finances with clear and immediate insights.
## Scope
### MVP
- Manual transaction input
- CSV import
- Monthly balance overview
- Expense categorization

### Future
- Multi-format support (PDF, XLSX)
- Investment tracking
- Smart insights and recommendations
- User authentication and authorization  
- Data privacy and compliance (e.g., LGPD)
